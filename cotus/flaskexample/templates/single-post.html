How to use this page:
To interpret each topic, the grey bars show the words that appear in these tweets most frequently.
It may seem like a puzzle, but on a good day, the theme of these topics can be discerned from this plot.
For each topic, you will also see how engaged the different politcal parties are,
as well as how this topic has trended over the last seven days.
Finally, the table presents a list of all of the Senators and Reps that had a tweet categorized into this topic.

About

Congress Tweets was dreamed up and developed in three whirlwind weeks by Adam Brown during his time as a Fellow at Insight Data Science. Before attempting to model the topics that Congress tweets about, Adam earned a PhD in Psychology from Northeastern University studying how social pressures influence motivation and performance.
